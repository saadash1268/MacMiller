A Little introduction to Mac

Mac Miller was an American rapper, singer and record producer from Pittsburgh. He was born on January 19, 1992 and passed away on September 7, 2018. Mac Miller was known for his unique style of music, which blended elements from jazz, soul and hip-hop.

Known for his smooth flow, witty lyrics and infectious beats. Throughout his career, Mac miller released several critically acclaimed albums including "Blue Slide Park", "Watching Movies with the Sound Off" and "The Feminine Divine".

"Mac Miller was one of the most talented musicians of our generation. He was a true artist that inspired so many people." - Kendrick Lamar
